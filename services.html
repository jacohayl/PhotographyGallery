Frequently Asked Questions

Most weekends and many weekday evenings are available. Contact me to check current availability!
Edited galleries are typically delivered within 2–3 weeks after your session (rush options available).
I am flexible when problems arise. If there is a technical error on my end, more than likely you will recieve a discount or money back. Having a second shooter for events is an option but will be more expensive.
You can request prints for an additional small fee (mainly just to cover material cost) - books, albumns, cards, etc.
I'm usually pretty flexible, but I would prefer at least a 24 hour notice. A cancellation or reschedule that is less than two hours before scheduled will result in a fee. A late fee may be a result if more late than a reasonable amount of time.
I work in South-Eastern Idaho but am willing to travel. The first approximate 50 miles is free, outside this radius will result in an additional $0.70–$1.50 per mile. If I am already in an area (for example I visit Utah frequently) the travel fee will be removed. However, if more costly travel expenses are required (such as hotel fee) this will be added to the cost of the shoot.
