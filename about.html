About Me

Hi, I'm Haylee — a photographer and graphic designer living and working in the South-Eastern Idaho area.

I find inspiration in the quiet beauty of the mountains, the changing seasons, and the people around me. Whether I'm behind the lens capturing raw emotion or in front of the screen crafting visual concepts, my goal is always the same: to create work that feels genuine, timeless, and meaningful.

One thing I value is hard work. I graduated high school with an associate degree, served a full-time (approx. 19½ months) proselyting mission, and am a spring 2026 graduate from Idaho State University with a bachelor's degree in visual communication (design track). I have always loved art and technology and decided to combine them, working toward developing the Godly characteristic of creativity.

What I love working on:

  • Creating graphics for digital and print media
  • Lifestyle & portrait photography
  • Media editing and manipulation
  • Social media management
  • Web design


Tools I use the most: Affinity, Adobe (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver), CapCut, Visual Studio, Canva, Figma
